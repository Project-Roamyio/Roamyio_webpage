Delete Account & Data

Last updated: 11-08-2025

To request deletion of your Roamyio account and associated personal data, please email us at info@roamyio.com from your registered email address or include your registered phone number.

What will be deleted

  • Account information (name, phone number, email)
  • Location history and saved places
  • App preferences and non-essential logs

What may be retained (and why)

  • Transaction or compliance records that we are legally required to keep (e.g., fraud prevention, audits)

Retention period: up to 180 days unless a longer period is required by law.

How to submit a request

  1. Send an email to info@roamyio.com with subject “Account Deletion Request”.
  2. Include your registered phone number or email and (optional) a brief reason.
  3. We’ll verify your identity and confirm once deletion is completed.

Timeline

We process verified deletion requests within 30 days.
