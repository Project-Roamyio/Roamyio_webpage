Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11-08-2025

Roamyio, operated by Valiant Siana Innovations Pvt. Ltd., values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email (for login and account creation).
  • Location Data: Real-time location for pickup/drop, map display, and service personalization.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and app usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide cab booking and other location-based services.
  • To improve app functionality and user experience.
  • To communicate updates, offers, or support messages.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Data may be shared with trusted service providers (e.g., Google Maps API) to operate app features.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure storage to protect your data.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at info@roamyio.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, contact us immediately.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the app means you accept the changes.
